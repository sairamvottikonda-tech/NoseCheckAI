Methodology

Measurement Protocol: Septal deviation measured in 3D Slicer on axial CT slices. Midline reference drawn from anterior nasal spine to crista galli. Maximum horizontal displacement of the septum from this midline recorded in millimeters.

Photo Pairing: Frontal face views rendered from CT volumes using 3D Slicer's Volume Rendering module with CT-Skin preset. These renderings show external facial structure derived from the same CT data, enabling paired comparison of internal deviation vs external asymmetry measured by NoseCheck.

Ethics

All CT data used are de-identified and publicly available through established research repositories (TCIA, Zenodo, Mendeley Data). No Institutional Review Board (IRB) approval is required for analysis of publicly available, de-identified data.
